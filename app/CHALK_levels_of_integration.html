Area, Riemann Sums, Integration formulas, Riemann integrability, and Lebesgue/Measure Theoretic Integrability are the same mathematical beast through different lenses at different levels of mathematical understanding and rigor. Usually you'd spend several years learning about these topics, and because of that, I decided to talk about them all in one video to try and connect/motivate each level based on the others.
